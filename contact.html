Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I donate to Al Iman Foundation?

You can donate to Al Iman Foundation through our online donation portal, bank transfer, or by visiting our office. All donations are tax-deductible. For more information, please visit our Donation page or contact our finance department.

When does registration for the new academic year open?

Registration for the new academic year typically opens in January and continues until April. Early registration is recommended as spots fill quickly. Please check the School page for specific dates and application procedures.

How can I volunteer at Al Iman Foundation?

We welcome volunteers in various areas including teaching, administration, event organization, and community outreach. Please fill out the volunteer form on our website or contact our volunteer coordinator for more information.

Do you offer scholarships for students?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for qualified students. Scholarship applications are typically reviewed alongside admission applications. Please contact our admissions office for more details about eligibility and application procedures.
